# Process Requirements (Workflow / Administrative)

Scope: Timelines, approvals, and procedural constraints (faculty + course).

## 1) Supervisor and topic
- A supervisor must accept the topic and the thesis must be registered according to the faculty process (Annex 1/2).

## 2) Mandatory deliverables (typical ETTI process)
- Interim progress uploads (semester milestones) as requested by the faculty platform (single PDF per stage).
- Final submission package:
  - thesis PDF (single file, cover → last annex)
  - poster PDF (required by faculty submission flow, if applicable)

## 3) File naming and upload
- Final thesis PDF filename must follow:
  - `FamilyName_Name1[_Name2...].pdf`
- Upload must be done within the announced window; missing deadlines typically blocks defense in that session.

## 4) Scientific Research 3 (SR3) constraints (your course)
- Prepare a document presenting the research topic using the ETTI dissertation template.
- Coordinator(s) must email the course owner with the proposed mark.
- Send the SR3 document by email no later than **26.01.2026**.
- Present the work in an online meeting no later than **30.01.2026**, starting from 17:00, for 5 minutes.

## 5) Internal working rules (for this project)
- Keep the thesis compiling at all times.
- Track requirements in three separate documents:
  - formatting vs. technical vs. procedural
- Every experiment must be logged and reproducible (config + seed + checkpoint).
- Every claim in the thesis must map to evidence (result table/figure/citation).
